# Mathematics, Typeset with MathJax

Vinary Viewer renders LaTeX math to crisp SVG via MathJax — inline and display,
in Markdown prose and in `$$`-fenced blocks.

## Inline

Euler's identity $`e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0`$ threads through the text at the baseline, as
does a fraction like $`\tfrac{1}{2}`$ or a subscript $`a_{n+1}`$.

## Display

The Gaussian integral:

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2}\,dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$

The Basel problem:

$$
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6}
$$

A 2×2 determinant:

$$
\det\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} = ad - bc
$$
